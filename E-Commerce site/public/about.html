Logistics

Shipping Policy

How long does shipping take?

Orders are dispatched within 2–3 business days. Delivery takes 5–8 business days depending on your location across India.

Is there free shipping?

Yes — free shipping on all orders above ₹249. Orders below ₹249 carry a flat ₹49 shipping fee.

Do you ship across India?

Yes, we deliver pan India including remote pin codes. Delivery timelines may vary for certain areas.

Policy

Returns & Refunds

Can I return a product?

We accept returns only if the product is damaged or defective on arrival. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos.

How do refunds work?

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

What about custom orders?

Custom orders are non-refundable unless there is a production error on our part.
